SafeMed - Drug Calculators

1. Basic Dose

(Want / Have) × Volume

2. IV Infusion Rate

Volume / Time

3. Drops per Minute

(Volume × Drop factor) / Time (minutes)

4. Weight-Based Dose

Weight × Dose per kg

5. Clark’s Rule

(Weight in lbs / 150) × Adult dose

6. BSA-Based Dose

(BSA / 1.73) × Adult dose

7. Percent Solution

% × 10 × Volume

8. Insulin Dose

Total carbs / Carb ratio

9. Reconstitution Dose

Prescribed dose / Concentration

10. Heparin Infusion

(Weight × Dose per kg/hr) / Concentration

11. Titration Dosing

Start at base dose, increase until max
