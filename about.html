About the Author

Chika Asogwa is a Health Informatics trainee with the NHS. His passion for creating DoseCalc comes from his journey through healthcare, tech, and compassion for students navigating the clinical environment.

He believes every nursing student should have simple, mobile access to essential calculations – especially during placement stress or exam crunch.

This tool was built to empower learning, reduce mistakes, and build confidence in real-life care scenarios.

Built by Chika Asogwa for every nursing student
